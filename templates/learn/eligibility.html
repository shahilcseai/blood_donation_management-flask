Blood Donation Eligibility

Basic Eligibility Requirements

  • Age: Must be at least 17 years old in most states
  • Weight: At least 110 pounds
  • Health: Generally good health and feeling well
  • Frequency: Wait at least 56 days between whole blood donations

Health Conditions

Most medications do not disqualify you from donating blood. Common medications like:
  • Blood pressure medications
  • Birth control pills
  • Antidepressants
  • Diabetic medications
are acceptable. However, some medications may require a waiting period.

Recent Activities

You should not donate if you have:

  • ✗ Had a cold or flu in the last 3 days
  • ✗ Received a tattoo in the last 3 months
  • ✗ Traveled to certain countries recently
  • ✗ Had major surgery in the last 6 months
