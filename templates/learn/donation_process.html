The Blood Donation Process

What to Expect

1. Registration

You'll need to:

  • Present valid ID
  • Complete basic health questionnaire
  • Provide contact information

2. Health Screening

A healthcare professional will:

  • Check your temperature
  • Measure blood pressure
  • Test hemoglobin levels
  • Review your health history

3. The Donation

The actual donation process:

  • Takes about 10-15 minutes
  • Collects approximately one pint of blood
  • Is performed by trained professionals
  • Uses sterile, single-use equipment

4. Refreshments & Recovery

After donation:

  • Rest for 15 minutes
  • Enjoy provided refreshments
  • Schedule your next donation

Before You Donate

  • ✓ Get plenty of sleep
  • ✓ Eat a healthy meal
  • ✓ Drink extra water
  • ✓ Bring ID and medication list

After Donation Care

  • ✓ Keep bandage on for 4-5 hours
  • ✓ Avoid heavy lifting
  • ✓ Stay hydrated
  • ✓ Eat iron-rich foods
